DATIM ETL Priorities and Communication Plan

Special Requests

Last updated: 06/24/19

Special requests are mainly received through the DATIM email and can also be requested via the Contact Us page in DATIM.

As long as the DATIM DataMart team does not run into any issues with loading the data for a particular state, then the state can usually be loaded within 1-2 business days. If we encounter issues with the data load, we first investigate and if needed we reach out for assistance with correcting the data in FIADB.

We keep the requestor informed with any issues we are dealing with and when we think they will be resolved.
