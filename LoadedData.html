Loaded Data

The DATIM team prioritizes data extractions, transformations, and loads (ETLs) based on the Plan Revision Statuses for NFS (i.e., "Needs (older than 15 years)," "Needs soon (10-15 years old)," "Under Revision," and "Revised within 10 years").

The table below sets forth data ETL'ed to the DATIM data mart for State inventories.

When a new state inventory is loaded/reloaded to the DATIM data mart, it will be added to the table below.
